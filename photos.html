Climate Club Pictures!

Below are some examples of what we get up to:

Making Top Trumps

We chose a list of 24 countries and populated a google-spreadsheet with all the info. It was great fun to see the numbers fill in as all the kids worked on this in parallel. We looked up the hottest and coldest records for each place, and also the annual rainfall. Then we added population and area statistics and finally the amount fo CO2 emitted by each country (using data from the great Carbon Atlas)

World Map of Impacts

We made some posters about the impacts of climate change on different regions, ecosystems and animals. Put them on a map of the world for the display board

Our Climate History

We used coloured lollipop sticks to make a chart of how the world has warmed - inspired by the Warming Stripes. This shows how we've warmed over the last century or so - but what next? What future shall we choose? we still have choices to fix Climate Change

Stripe our School!

Exminster school were so inspired by Climate Club that we put some Warming Stripes on the school!
