Climate Club

Free Educational Climate resources for schools and clubs

Welcome to Climate Club!

Climate Club is a STEM outreach activity which aims to provide free teaching resources for schools and clubs to learn about climate and climate change - it's causes, impacts and solutions.

On these pages you will be able to find presentations and slides and lesson plans to help teachers and volunteers set-up and run lessons or after-schol clubs on Climate Change

Content has been written for and is mainly intended for years 5 and 6 in UK schools - approximately ages 10-11

  • On the "About" Climate Club pages you will find explanation in more detail of the format and background of Climate Club, including some short intro videos giving specific information to teachers, parents and children about what Climate Club is for you
  • About Chris Jones is a brief bio of me and my science background
  • The "Resources" page is where you find all the material, slides, lesson plans etc to be able to run your own Climate Club
  • "Photos" show some pictures of examples of our work, displays etc.
  • Climate Club resources are free to use, see "Terms" for more details
