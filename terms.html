Climate Club terms, permissions and notes on usage

The intention is that everything on this site is completely free to download and use. Our goal is to provide ready and reliable information to help run lessons and clubs about climate change

It is licensed under a Creative Commons licence which means you are free to download and use in classrooms etc, to reproduce it freely and to adapt and edit it, and also re-share. But there should be no commercial use of this. I do not sell or profit from this activity and it is my intention that it is always free-to-use. See links at bottom of page for more details on Creative Commons CC-BY-NC-SA-4 licence.
